OUR STORY

About Us

One Big Dream

It all started with one big dream, two crazy brothers, and the perfect combination of flour, sugar, and chocolate chips. The Cookie Factorie was co-founded by Ron Herns (CEO) & Peyton Wijnaldum (COO). They both teamed up and dove head-first into the world of baking. After thousands of dollars in wasted dough, recipes that did not live up to their expectations, and cookies that are just plain embarrassing to them today, the two brothers decided to take their 'perfect cookie quest' to the people. They gathered feedback and tested recipes — a practice that is still part of the The Factorie process.
